Help & Support

Find answers to your questions and get the support you need.

To create an account, click on the "Get Started" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you will receive instructions to reset your password.

To place an order, browse the products on our marketplace, add the desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in the required details and confirm your order.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI. Choose the payment method that suits you best during the checkout process.

If you need assistance, you can contact our customer support team via the "Contact Us" page. Fill out the form with your query, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
